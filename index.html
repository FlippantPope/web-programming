Initial Landing Page

This is the first index page for a site that will be about upcoming video game releases.

Hello, my name is Jon Middleton. I am originally from Florida and currently live in Northern California. Growing up, computers always piqued my interest. I remember being introduced to them at a young age when dad brought home a Gateway desktop, where most applications were run from 2.5-inch floppy disks instead of stored on the local hard drive. That is when I learned about video games.

Fast forward almost 30 years, not only has my life changed significantly but so has the video game industry. Now the video game industry is worth $90 billion, according to statista.com [1], and has made its way into almost every living room, including my own. Over the years, the type of video games I play have varied from first-person shooters to farming simulators. How I play video games has also varied. For a long time, I played on console platforms such as Xbox or PlayStation. But more recently I have gone back to my roots and have been focusing on PC gaming.

In the future, this website will be iterated on with features and content that will focus on providing information and updates for upcoming video game releases. A short how-to guide will be created to showcase the steps needed to get updates for video games on the Steam storefront. Also, dedicated pages will be created to showcase a few video games that are already more popular.
