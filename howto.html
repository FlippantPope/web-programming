Steam Wishlist

All the information you could ever need.

A good way to stay up to date with information about games that have not been released yet, is using a Steam wishlist. Steam wishlists allow you to view information about updates for upcoming releases. Below is a video on how to use the Steam Storefront to add a game to your wishlist.

Steps from video

Using Steam Search

  1. From Steam Storefront, use search bar at top right.
  2. Click Game to wishlist.
  3. On Game's Store page scroll past videos and images
  4. Click Add to Wishlist

Using Steam Frontpage

  1. From Steam Storefront, click game that you want to wishlist.
  2. On Game's Store page scroll past videos and images
  3. Click Add to Wishlist
